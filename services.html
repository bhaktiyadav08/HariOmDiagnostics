Pathology Services

Comprehensive diagnostic services including routine blood tests, thyroid and diabetes profiles, hormone assays, urine & stool examination, health check-up packages and home sample collection.

Blood Tests

Routine and advanced blood investigations for screening, diagnosis and monitoring.

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR)
  • Blood Sugar - Fasting / PP / Random
  • Lipid Profile (Cholesterol, Triglycerides, HDL, LDL)
  • Liver Function Tests (LFT)
  • Kidney Function Tests (KFT / RFT)
  • Serum Electrolytes
  • Serum Uric Acid

Thyroid Profile

Thyroid function tests for evaluation and monitoring of thyroid disorders.

  • Thyroxine (T4)
  • Triiodothyronine (T3)
  • Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH)
  • Extended thyroid panels (as per doctor advice)

Diabetes Tests

Tests to diagnose, monitor and manage diabetes effectively.

  • Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS)
  • Post Prandial Blood Sugar (PPBS)
  • Random Blood Sugar
  • Glycated Hemoglobin (HbA1c)
  • Urine Sugar & Ketones

Hormone & Vitamin Tests

Specialized tests for hormones and vitamins, as advised by your doctor.

  • Vitamin B12
  • Vitamin D (25-OH)
  • Testosterone
  • Other hormone assays (on request)

Urine & Stool Examination

Routine and microscopic examination to help detect infections and other conditions.

  • Urine Routine & Microscopy
  • Stool Routine & Microscopy
  • Occult Blood (if applicable)
