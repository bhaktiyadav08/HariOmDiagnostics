About Hariom Diagnostics

Hariom Pathology Laboratory / Hariom Diagnostics is a trusted diagnostic center in Akurdi, Pune, known for accurate and affordable pathology services with patient-first care.

Who We Are

Hariom Pathology Laboratory is committed to providing reliable diagnostic reports that help doctors and patients make the right health decisions. We combine modern analyzers, strict quality control and experienced staff to deliver consistent and trustworthy results.

We offer routine and advanced blood tests, full body health check-up packages, hormone tests, diabetes and thyroid profiles, urine & stool examination and home sample collection for patients across Akurdi and nearby areas.
